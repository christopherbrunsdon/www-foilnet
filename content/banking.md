## Important

- Please use your **full name and appointment date** as the payment reference so we can allocate your payment correctly.
- Send your **proof of payment** to [info@foilnet.co.za](mailto:info@foilnet.co.za) or WhatsApp it to +27 82 000 0000.
- EFT payments must be received and confirmed **24 hours before** your appointment.
- Deposits of 50 % are required for bookings over R 500.

## In-Salon Payments

We accept the following in-salon payment methods:

- Cash (exact change appreciated)
- Visa & Mastercard (credit and debit)
- SnapScan
- Zapper
